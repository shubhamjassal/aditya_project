Present State of AI

Artificial intelligence (AI) has become a technological reality for businesses and organizations across industries. Even if its benefits may not be always easy to quantify, AI has proven itself capable of improving process efficiency, reducing human errors and labor, and extracting insights from big data. In 2019, AI adoption among large companies has increased by 47% compared to 2018, according to the latest Artificial Intelligence Index report.
